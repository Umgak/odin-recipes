Lohikeitto

Image credit: Lillian Rosenberg (that's me!)

Description

Lohikeitto is a Finnish salmon chowder, made with potatoes, carrots, and leek!

It's creamy, hearty, and always delicious!

This is Lily's favorite recipe, it was given to her by a close friend.

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbsp of unsalted butter
  • 600 mL fish stock
  • 150 mL heavy cream
  • 1/4 of a leek, chopped
  • 2 medium carrots, chopped
  • 1 medium potato, peeled and cubed
  • 2 skinless salmon fillets, cubed
  • 2 Tbsp dill, finely chopped

Instructions

  1. Melt the butter in a medium saucepan, then sauté the leek in the butter over medium heat until it begins to turn translucent
  2. Add the carrots, potato, and fish stock to the saucepan, increase to high heat, and let cook for 8 minutes, stirring occasionally
  3. Add the salmon and heavy cream, lower to medium heat, and let cook for 8 minutes, stirring occasionally
  4. Remove from heat, add dill and let cool for 10 minutes. Add pepper to taste
  5. Serve and enjoy

Makes about 2-3 servings.

Serving suggestions

I've experimented with adding a small amount of lemon juice to the finished product with some success. Give it a try!

Dunking sourdough bread into the broth is delicious.
