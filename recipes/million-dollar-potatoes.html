Million Dollar Twice-Baked Potatoes

Image credit: Brenda Venable, ALLRECIPES

Description

These million dollar twice-baked potatoes are creamy and rich. Potatoes are stuffed with Boursin and Cheddar cheeses, sour cream, cream, butter, bacon and green onions; they are also great to make ahead!

Recipe shamelessly lifted from Allrecipes. It looked tasty.

Ingredients

  • 4 large baking potatoes
  • 4 teaspoons olive oil
  • 3/4 cup sour cream
  • 1 package spreadable gourmet cheese
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese, divided
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt, or to taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 4 slices bacon, cooked until crisp and crumbled
  • 2 green onions, thinly sliced or 3 tablespoons snipped fresh chives
  • fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Gather ingredients and preheat oven to 400°F.
  2. Scrub potatoes and dry with a clean cloth or paper towel, oil in olive oil.
  3. Bake potatoes directly on the oven rack in the center of the oven until fork tender, about 1 hour. Reduce oven temperature to 375°F.
  4. Allow potatoes to cool long enough to handle. Cut in half and scoop out interior flesh into a large bowl, being careful not to tear the skins. Set skins on a sheet pan.
  5. Mash potatoes in the bowl, and stir in cream, gourmet cheese, melted butter, half the shredded cheese, and cream. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Use all the potato mixture to stuff the reserved potato skins, mounding up as needed.
  7. Bake at 375°F until heated through, about 20 minutes.
  8. Sprinkle with remaining cheese, and top with bacon and green onions or chives. Garnish with parsley and serve hot.
